Innovative Training

“There’s no ‘wax on, wax off’ with DevOps. Learn by doin’ it.”.

Our program teaches DevOps by having unpaid interns accomplish DevOps tasks. The first steps of the program are rather traditional establishing a solid foundation of skills required for DevOps.

After displaying a solid understanding of the foundational skills for DevOps interns will participate in scrums as a DevOps engineer for iDevOps completing daily DevOps sprint tasks learning how to be an effective DevOps engineer. Interns will also study and schedule certification exams, all certifications must be complete before completion of the program and placement can happen.

Certifications:

  • Python from Expert Rating.
  • Scrum Master.
  • AWS Certified SysOps Administrator Associate.
  • AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate.
  • AWS Certified DevOps Engineer Professional.
  • AWS Certified Solutions Architect Professional.

Why would you hire a DevOps engineer from iDevOps?

We believe in teaching people by having them do exactly what they would be doing in the real world.

We teach a method that allows interns to complete real world DevOps tasks they have no experience in. They learn a method of research and approach to solving DevOps tasks. We do this in order to assure that no matter what technology is thrown at them, they can quickly become and expert and complete the tasks. We teach a method of approach to solving problems that teaches adaptability.
